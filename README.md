# Wildfire Risk 

### This map shows the wildfire risk for all counties in the US, the counties are colored based on choropleth shading with the higher risk counties being in warming colors and the lower risk counties being in cooler colors. Clicking the counties will also pull up a popup that shows that counties indivdual risk rating. The map also has induvial points with fire icons that show the top 15 most devastating wildfires of 2025. Clickinhg on these icons will show more info about every fire.
